Our Story

We wake at 3am so you don't have to.

La Mie Dorée was born in a small kitchen in Lyon, where founder Marie Lefebvre learned that bread-making is patience made edible. Every morning before dawn, our bakers begin the ritual: flour, water, salt, time.

No shortcuts. No preservatives. Just craft passed down through three generations and the kind of crust that sings when you break it.
